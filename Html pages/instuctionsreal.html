Q. What are the new IRCTC rules for passengers
A: In light of COVID-19, the IRCTC has issued some new rules and safety precautions for passengers travelling on trains. These rules state that:

• Only passengers with confirmed and RAC tickets will be allowed to board the train.

• Face masks and hand hygiene are mandatory for all passengers.

• Passengers have to reach the railway station at least 90 minutes before the scheduled departure of the train.

• Social distancing is mandatory at the station and also on the train.

• On arrival, passengers will have to follow the health protocols prescribed by the destination state.

• Catering Service is not available and catering charges not included in the fare

• No blanket and linen shall be provided in the train.

Online Train Ticket Booking

Here’s how to book IRCTC tickets online:

• Open the ixigo website or download mobile app. Click on book train tickets section.

• Select the source and destination Indian Railway stations from the list.

• Pick a date of departure and travel class- AC First Class, Sleeper Class, Second AC, Third AC, AC Chair car.

• Choose your Indian Railway train and travel class based on arrivel time and travel time (in hours)

• Fill in the passenger details including berth perference, names of children, senior citizens.

• Log in using your IRCTC user ID and password. Create a new IRCTC username if you don't have one.

• Complete the payment and reservation process using your IRCTC login details. You will be redirected to IRCTC website.

• You will then receive a confirmation on your registered email id and SMS on mobile number.
